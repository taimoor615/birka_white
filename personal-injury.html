Personal Injury

Birka-White Law Offices represents individuals who have suffered serious injuries in a wide variety of accidents including, but not limited to the following:

  • automobile accidents
  • automobile v. pedestrian accidents
  • premises liability (e.g., slip and fall)

Automobile accidents are usually the result of the carelessness or negligence of a driver who has committed one of the following acts:

  • speeding
  • turning in front of oncoming traffic
  • failure to yield the right of way
  • drunk driving
  • inattentiveness (i.e., cell phone)

Our attorneys understand the burden and inconvenience that an injury can cause you and your family. We will work to recover the maximum award for your injuries and economic losses including past and future medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering.

If you have been injured in an accident, call our firm toll-free 866-474-9999 for a free consultation.
