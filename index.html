OUR FIRM

Birka-White Law Offices is a boutique law firm in Northern California, specializing in complex civil litigation and criminal defense. Founded in 1982 by David Birka-White, Birka-White Law Office quickly grew from a local law practice into a national powerhouse. Birka-White Law Offices consistently battles the biggest companies in the world, and wins.

The firm has obtained major settlements on behalf of individuals in hundreds of cases involving defective products in the building, insurance, and energy industries. To date, Birka-White Law Offices has recovered nearly half a billion dollars on behalf of clients. Over the years, the practice has expanded to include product liability, personal injury, consumer fraud, business and regulatory, and criminal defense.

Attorneys

David M. Birka-White

David Birka-White is the founding member of Birka-White Law Offices. David opened the office in 1982, in San Ramon, Calif. Since then, Birka-White Law Offices has successfully sued hundreds of Fortune and Global 500 companies in complex civil cases, including State Farm, British Petroleum, Whirlpool, and Bosch, among many others. While Birka-White Law Offices has always been based in the Bay Area, the firm represents clients around the world.

In recent years, David has worked to expand the firms practice areas beyond class actions, into personal injury, business and regulatory, consumer fraud, and products liability.
