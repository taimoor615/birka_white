Class Action

Class actions are designed to resolve complex lawsuits involving large numbers of individuals. Often, the claim of a single class member, even though significant, is not large enough to economically justify individual litigation. Thus, where the nature of each personâ€™s claim has overlapping common issues, we seek court approval to treat the case as a class action. In this way, thousands of people can be represented and helped in a case in which they do not need to be a named party. The named plaintiffs are referred to as class representatives.

Class representatives work with the attorneys during the course of the case as is necessary. The time commitment of a class representative is reasonable and the experience rewarding. At the conclusion of every settled case, Birka-White Law Offices requests that the Court grant a reasonable payment, to the class representative for their valuable contribution to the class members as a whole.
